what makes our coffee special!

Nestled within the historic Galle Fort, KiXi Café offers a unique blend of colonial charm and modern aesthetics. Since its inception in 2020, we've been dedicated to providing high-quality coffee blends and delectable pastries inspired by both local and international flavors. Our signature "teddy coffees" and delightful milkshakes have become favorites among both locals and travelers. Experience the enchanting ambiance of our multi-level café, where every visit promises a memorable culinary journey.
